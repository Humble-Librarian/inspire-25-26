Techno Minders

Event Category

Group(4)

Event Brief

  • Team-based competition (Maths, Physics & Chemistry, 11th–12th level).
  • 5 rounds, each with 3–4 questions, increasing in difficulty.
  • Each correct answer gives one digit of a 6-digit password.
  • Winner = team that submits the correct password in sequence.
  • Penalty system: 1 penalty = +20 sec to total time.
  • Tests speed, accuracy, problem-solving, and knowledge application.
  • Only simple calculators allowed; can be used once for 60 seconds, extra time incurs penalty (every 10 sec = +20 sec).

Rules and Regulations

  • Each team must have 4 members.
  • Problems given in sequence, no skipping allowed.
  • Wrong digit submission = 0.5 penalty.
  • Only one member can use a calculator at a time.
  • Team discussion allowed, but discipline required.
  • Answers must follow the given format.
  • Mobiles are not allowed.
  • Malpractice = disqualification.
  • Maintain discipline and sportsmanship.

Judging Criteria

  • Winner = team that submits the 5-digit password in least time.
  • Tie-breaker = decided by penalty count.
  • Accuracy over speed is prioritized.
  • Discipline, teamwork, and rule adherence matter.
  • Judges’ decision is final.
