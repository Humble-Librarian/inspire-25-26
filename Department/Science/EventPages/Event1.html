Math-O-Run

Event Category

Group(4)

Event Brief

  • Exciting team-based mathematics challenge.
  • Tests speed, accuracy, logical reasoning, and teamwork.
  • Follows a relay race format – each player must solve their challenge before the next can begin.
  • After the first five challenges, the event ends with a rapid-fire round.
  • The rapid-fire round decides the ultimate winner.

Round Structure

  • Round 1 – Relay Challenges
    • 5 members per team.
    • Relay style: next player starts after previous finishes.
    • 5 challenges (30–90 sec each). Total time: ~5 mins.
    • Calculator allowed (penalty –9/10 points).
    • Bonus: Finish early = extra points (–1 point / 4 sec saved).
  • Round 2 – Rapid Fire
    • 10 questions to both teams simultaneously.
    • 5 sec to answer each.

Scoring:

  • 1st team correct: +7 | Wrong: –3
  • Passed to 2nd team: Correct: +4 | Wrong: –2
  • No answer: Skipped

Rules and Regulations

  • Each team must have 5 participants.
  • Relay challenges must be completed in sequence.
  • Misconduct or unfair means = disqualification.
  • Calculator use is penalized as per rules.
  • Judges’ decision is final and binding.

Judging Criteria

  • Teams must complete all five relay challenges within the time frame.
  • The Rapid Fire round determines the winner based on points.
  • In case of a tie, the time taken in the relay round will be the deciding factor.
