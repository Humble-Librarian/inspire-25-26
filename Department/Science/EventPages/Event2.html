Event Category

Individual

Event Brief

  • A real-life placement simulator designed to test skills, knowledge, and mindset.
  • Event consists of five rounds:
    • Aptitude Test
    • Group Discussion
    • Technical Interview
    • HR Interview
    • Pitch the Idea Round

Round Structure

  • 1. Aptitude Test
    • Assesses logical reasoning, quantitative aptitude, and verbal skills.
    • Focus on speed, accuracy, and problem-solving under time constraints.
  • 2. Group Discussion
    • Platform to express ideas and opinions on given topics.
    • Evaluates communication, teamwork, leadership, and group dynamics handling.
  • 3. Technical Interview
    • Tests branch-specific knowledge.
    • Assesses problem-solving, analytical thinking, and concept application.
  • 4. HR Interview
    • Focuses on personality, attitude, confidence, and professional behavior.
    • Helps participants learn how to present themselves effectively to recruiters.
  • 5. Pitch the Idea
    • Shortlisted participants act as interviewers and present innovative ideas/solutions.
    • Evaluates creativity, leadership, presentation, and strategic thinking.

Judging Criteria

  • Aptitude Test: Accuracy, speed, logical reasoning ability.
  • Group Discussion: Clarity of thought, communication, confidence, teamwork.
  • Technical Interview: Subject knowledge, problem-solving, practical application.
  • HR Interview: Attitude, personality, ethics, adaptability.
  • Pitch-the-Idea: Originality, feasibility, clarity, convincing ability.
  • Professionalism & Presentation: Grooming, etiquette, overall conduct.
