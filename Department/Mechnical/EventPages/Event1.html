Junkyard Wars:
Scrap To Innovation

Event Category

Group(2-4)

Event Brief

  • Material Usage:
    • Only materials provided from the junkyard may be used.
    • Points are awarded based on proper usage; exceeding limits leads to penalties.
    • External/purchased materials are strictly prohibited.
  • Object Construction:
    • Build an object similar to the sample provided using junkyard materials.
    • Replication is necessary to qualify for the next round.
    • Unique and innovative designs earn additional creativity points.
    • If no team clears the qualifying test, innovation and creativity will be considered for qualification.
  • Time Limit:
    • Complete design and construction within the prescribed time.
    • Finishing early earns bonus points.
    • Exceeding the time limit results in penalties impacting the final score.

Rules and Regulations

  • Team event (min:2 and max:4 persons).
  • Teams will receive mystery Junk Components based on theme.
  • May use it to build desired object.
  • Teams must demonstrate their object functionality upon completion.

Judging Criteria

  • Creativity and originality
  • Technical skill
  • Performance
  • Functionality
  • Time
