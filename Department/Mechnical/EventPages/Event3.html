Quiz:
Think & Win

Rules

  • Quiz Consist of three rounds:
    • Elimination - Easy Question.
    • Advanced - Medium Level Question.
    • Final - Tough Question.
  • Limited time per question (30 sec).
  • No use of mobile, tabs, laptop or any wearables.
  • Team of (3-4) participants are allowed in 1 team.

Question Format & Difficulty Level

Quest. Type Easy Medium Hard
Correct Ans +2 +4 +6
Wrong Ans -1 -3 -5
Un attempted 0 0 0

Tie-Breaker System

  • The team with the least negative points ranks higher.
  • Correct ans. +5 points, wrong ans -2. & Unattempted 0 points.
  • The Team with more fastest-answer bonuses ranks higher +1 point. [In less than 5 sec].

Disqualification Criteria

  • If any team reaches NEGATIVE total points, they are eliminated.
  • Any Misconduct or cheating leads to instant disqualification.
  • The final Call is of Quiz Committee's.
