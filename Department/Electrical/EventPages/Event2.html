DND(Debug & Design)

Event Category

Group(2)

Event Brief

  • Name: Debug & Glow Challenge
  • Focus: Test logic, problem-solving, and practical electronics skills.
  • Format: Two rounds – Bug-finding & Hands-on circuit building.
  • Team Size: Duo (2 members per team)
  • Skills Tested: Circuit analysis, debugging, practical implementation, accuracy, and speed.
  • Materials Provided: Circuit diagrams, components, breadboards, and related materials.
  • Judging: Accuracy of circuit completion and minimum completion time.

Rules and Regulations

  • Team Size: Duo
  • Time Bound: Minimum time will be considered for ranking
  • No Devices: Mobile phones or internet strictly prohibited
  • Fair Play: Any malpractice or misconduct leads to immediate disqualification
  • Equipment: All required materials provided; participants must handle carefully
  • Judges’ Decision: Final and binding for all rounds

Round Structure

  • Round 1 – Debug a Thon

    • Description: Find bugs and mistakes in a provided simple circuit diagram.
    • Team Size: Duo
    • Time Limit: Minimum time considered
    • Elimination: Top 5 teams advance to Round 2
    • Venue: D2, second floor

  • Round 2 – Glitch to Glow

    • Description: Implement a real-time circuit on a breadboard using given statements and components.
    • Format: Practical task
    • Time Limit: Time-bound
    • Winning Criteria: Accuracy of circuit completion and correct functioning
