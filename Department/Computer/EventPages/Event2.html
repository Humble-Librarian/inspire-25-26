Techpitch

Event Category

Group (4)

Event Brief

  • Topics given on the spot through chits.
  • Teams must design smart, tech-based solutions for the assigned topic.
  • Prepare a presentation covering:
    • Logo & Tagline
    • Smart Features & Technology Stack
    • Target Audience
    • Marketing Agenda
    • Future Scope
    • Finance
  • Jury will ask Q&A on the project.

Rules and Regulations

  • Topics chosen randomly from the snippet bowl.
  • Presentation: 10–12 slides (must include intro & conclusion).
  • AI tools allowed for logo creation.
  • 1 hour prep time in a closed setting.
  • Teams must be present at their allocated time slot.
  • Each team gets 7–10 minutes for presentation + Q&A.
  • Presentation delivered to the jury panel.

Judging Criteria

  • Innovation & Creativity – originality, uniqueness, smart features
  • Feasibility & Practicality – realistic implementation
  • Presentation Skills – clarity, confidence, communication
  • Marketing Strategy – scalability, market application
  • Impact & Usefulness – solving real-world problems, lifestyle improvement
  • Scoring – Points awarded based on how well each criterion is met
