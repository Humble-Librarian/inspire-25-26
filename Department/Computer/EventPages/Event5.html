Flyer Fusion

Event Category

Individual / Group(2)

Event Brief

  • Focus on designing visually impactful flyers on technology and innovation.
  • Tests creativity, design skills, and artistic imagination.
  • Flyers must clearly convey the theme with strong visuals and messaging.
  • Participants can design flyers digitally.
  • Evaluation based on creativity, theme relevance, clarity, and overall presentation.

Rules and Regulations

  • Participation: Solo or Team (as per event guidelines).
  • Theme: Flyers must strictly follow the given theme/subtopics.
  • Originality: Only original designs; plagiarism or copyrighted material not allowed.
  • Format: Submit in A4 size as JPEG/PNG/PDF.
  • Time Limit: 1.5 hours (including 15 minutes for idea planning).
  • Tools: Participants must bring their own laptop and design software.
  • Disqualification: Any vulgar/offensive content, plagiarism, or AI-generated designs will lead to instant disqualification.

Round Structure

Round 1 – Easy Level

  • Format: 15 minutes for brainstorming, remaining time for designing.
  • Time Limit: 1.5 hours.
  • Scoring (100 points):
    • Creativity & Innovation – 30%
    • Relevance to Theme – 25%
    • Design & Aesthetic Appeal – 20%
    • Clarity of Message – 15%
    • Overall Impact – 10%
  • Elimination: Top 3 participants declared winners.

Tiebreaker Rule

Extra time will be given to tied participants for further improvement.

Judging Criteria

100 Points

  • Creativity & Innovation – 30 pts
  • Relevance to Theme – 25 pts
  • Design & Aesthetic Appeal – 20 pts
  • Clarity of Message – 15 pts
  • Overall Impact – 10 pts
