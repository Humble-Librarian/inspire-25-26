Smart City Planning

Event Category

Group (2–4)

Event Brief

  • Participants act as urban planners
  • Design a smart, sustainable town/city layout within the given time
  • Must apply real planning concepts:
    • Zoning
    • Utilities
    • Green Infrastructure
    • Transportation
    • Sustainability

Rules and Regulations

  • Team Size: 2–4 members
  • Time Limit: 2 hours (90 min planning + 30 min presentation & judging)
  • Materials Provided: Sheets, colors, and stationery (by organizers only)

Design Constraints

  • Minimum 20% area as Green Cover
  • Must include Smart Mobility (metro, cycle tracks, EV charging)
  • Provide Rainwater Harvesting & Waste Management
  • At least 1 Digital/IoT feature (e.g., smart grid, e-governance, traffic light)

Presentation

  • 5 minutes per team
  • Must cover: Zoning, Smart Features, Sustainability, Innovation

Judging Criteria

Scoring (100 Points):

  • Innovation & Smart Idea – 25 pts
  • Sustainability – 20 pts
  • Practicality – 20 pts
  • Adaptability – 15 pts
  • Layout & Presentation – 20 pts
