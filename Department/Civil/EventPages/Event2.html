Crane-o-vation

Event Category

Group Event (2–4 Members)

Event Brief

  • Teams must design and construct a mini crane boom.
  • Materials: Chopsticks and basic joining materials only.
  • The crane should be able to lift a small load at the tip.
  • It must not collapse, tip over, or break during the lift.

Rules and Regulations

  • Team Size: 2–4 members
  • Time Limit: 30–45 minutes for construction
  • Materials Provided: 40–50 chopsticks, rubber bands, masking tape, or thread
  • Materials to Bring: Scissors, string for hook suspension
  • Dimensions: Base = 20×20 cm, Boom length ≥ 30 cm from pivot
  • Load Test: Hook with string attached, loads added until failure/tipping
  • Restrictions: No glue, pre-made connectors, or external support

Judging Criteria (100 Marks)

  • Load Capacity: Max weight lifted – 40 pts
  • Stability: No wobble/tilt – 20 pts
  • Design & Aesthetics: Neatness, symmetry, load distribution – 15 pts
  • Material Efficiency: Strong with minimal material – 10 pts
  • Reach: Boom distance from base – 10 pts
  • Time Bonus: Early finish with quality – 5 pts
  • Total = 100 Points
